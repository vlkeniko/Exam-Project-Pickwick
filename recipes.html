Earl Grey cake

Ingredients

  • 6 bags of Pickwick Earl Grey
  • 1 liter of boiling water
  • 100 grams of unsalted butter + extra for greasing the cake tin
  • 4 eggs
  • 150 grams of dark caster sugar
  • 100 grams of cane sugar
  • 250 grams self-raising flour
  • 20 grams of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons of Greek yogurt
  • 150 grams of milk chocolate chips 
  • 50 grams of white chocolate
  • 150 grams of fruit such as blueberries or blackberries

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees. 
  2. Grease the baking pan and line it with baking paper. 
  3. Then start making tea. Hang the bags of Pickwick Earl Gray in 1 liter of boiling water for about 5 minutes. Then leave it to cool. 
  4. Mix the caster sugar, cane sugar and butter to a light mixture. 
  5. Add the eggs one at a time to the batter. Mix this well. 
  6. Add the Earl Gray and Greek yogurt to this. 
  7. Pass the self-raising flour and cocoa powder through the sieve and add the baking powder and baking soda. Then mix this through the batter as well. 
  8. Cut the white chocolate into cubes and add it to the batter together with 50 grams of the milk chocolate chips. 
  9. Pour the batter into the round baking pan. 
  10. Bake the cake for 35 minutes in the preheated oven. 
  11. When the cake is ready, spread the remaining 100 grams of chocolate chips over the cake. When the cake has cooled slightly, place it in the fridge to allow the chocolate to harden further.
  12. Top the cake with your favorite fruit! We use blueberries and blackberries. 

Strawberry Ice tea

Ingredients

  • 4 tea bags Pickwick Strawberry
  • 10 strawberries
  • 3 slices of watermelon
  • 4 teaspoons strawberry syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  • Couple of mint leaves
  • 500ml hot water
  • 500 ml ice cubes

Method

  1. Bring 500 ml of water to the boil.
  2. Let 4 bags of Pickwick Strawberry steep in the boiled water for 6-8 minutes.
  3. Take a pitcher and fill it with the ice cubes.
  4. Pour the hot tea in the pitcher over the ice cubes.
  5. Add the strawberry syrup or vanilla sugar.
  6. Let the iced tea cool*
  7. Garnish with strawberry pieces, watermelon and mint leaves.
  8. Time to enjoy!

Ginger tea

Ingredients

  • fresh ginger
  • optional: mint and honey

Method

  1. Boil water and meanwhile cut the ginger into pieces. If you want to be sure that the ginger is clean, first remove the skin with a potato peeler. 
  2. Put the boiling water in a cup and add the fresh ginger. 
  3. You can optionally add some mint and honey for more diverse flavors.
